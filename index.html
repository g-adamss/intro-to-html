About Me!

Hello! My name is Georgia Adams, and I am a first year student in the Graphic Design Program at Algonquin College! I am 19 going on 20 this year, and for a 10 of those 19 years I have been practicing with different mediums of art. I started to take art seriously when I was living in the United States for 3 years, and I took multiple art classes all throughout high school. I took a ga year after leaving university and soon after I applied to the Graphic Design Program with high hopes, and here I am!

My biggest supporters of my choice in pursing art would have to be my family and close friends. I originally applied to university for sociology, as I just wanted to get a degree, and I wasn’t actually pursuing something I enjoyed. However after taking a step back and realizing that art isn’t a useless career (as some people told me growing up) I decided to apply, and I am so glad that I did. Life is too short to be “spending” (wasting) time doing something that you don’t enjoy.

"I'm not going spend my life worrying about opinions from people who don't even know me."

Georgia Adams
